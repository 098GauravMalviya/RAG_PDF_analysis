# TENDER SPECIFICATION DOCUMENT
**Tender Reference Number:** TND-MOD-2026-8923
**Issuing Authority:** Ministry of Defence (MoD), Acquisition Wing, New Delhi
**Date of Release:** June 15, 2026
**Submission Deadline:** August 30, 2026

---

## SECTION I: SCOPE OF WORK & GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
The Ministry of Defence invites sealed bids from eligible domestic defense manufacturers for the supply of fifty (50) mobile launcher assemblies for tactical missile systems. The scope includes system design, fabrication, assembly, precision machining, electronics integration, extensive environmental stress testing, and on-site deployment guidance.

---

## SECTION II: TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
The supplier must manufacture and test the tactical missile launcher assemblies in compliance with the following standards:

### 2.1 Environmental and Structural Standards
1. **Operating Temperature:** The system must operate reliably under extreme climate conditions, specifically within a temperature range of -30°C to +75°C. Reference: Clause 2.1.1.
2. **Environmental Testing:** The complete assembly must be certified under MIL-STD-810H for vibration (up to 8g) and high-humidity environments. Reference: Clause 2.1.2.
3. **Materials:** Primary structural frames must utilize high-tensile domestic defense-grade steel. Proof of local sourcing is mandatory. Reference: Clause 2.1.3.

### 2.2 Electrical & Manufacturing Standards
1. **Soldering and Wiring:** All internal PCBs and cable assemblies must be manufactured in accordance with IPC/WHMA-A-620 and J-STD-001 military-grade soldering specifications. Reference: Clause 2.2.1.
2. **Quality Assurance:** The manufacturer's production facility must maintain AS9100 Rev D certification throughout the execution of the contract. Reference: Clause 2.2.2.

---

## SECTION III: FINANCIAL & CONTRACTUAL GUARANTEES
Bidders must satisfy the following financial constraints:

1. **Earnest Money Deposit (EMD):** A bank guarantee for Earnest Money Deposit of $1.5 Million USD must be submitted along with the technical bid. Reference: Clause 3.1.
2. **Performance Bank Guarantee (PBG):** The successful bidder will be required to submit a Performance Security equivalent to 10% of the total contract value within 30 days of contract signing. Reference: Clause 3.2.
3. **Ceiling Budget:** The total contract ceiling price is established at $12.5 Million USD. Bids exceeding this ceiling will be summarily rejected. Reference: Clause 3.3.

---

## SECTION IV: BIDDER ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
Only entities meeting the following criteria will be qualified for evaluation:

1. **Turnover:** The bidder must have an average annual turnover of at least $500 Million USD during the last three consecutive financial years. Reference: Clause 4.1.
2. **Past Performance:** Bidders must have successfully manufactured and delivered at least three (3) defense-grade mechanical or aerospace assemblies to government or aerospace agencies in the last five (5) years. Reference: Clause 4.2.
3. **Security Clearance:** Bidders must possess a valid Secret-level facility security clearance from the Department of Defence. Reference: Clause 4.3.
